<ComingSoon tier="v2" />

Time tracking itself is live — timers, manual entries, planned-versus-interruption
tagging, and roll-ups all work today; see the
[time tracking guide](/docs/guides/users/time-tracking). This page covers what's planned
on top of it.

One honest detail: every time entry **already carries a billable flag** — you can mark
entries billable today. What does not exist yet is everything that makes the flag pay
off: rates, costs, and invoicing are the planned part.

What's planned:

- **Billable rates and invoicing** — attach rates to the existing billable flag and bill from it (v2).
- **Idle detection / reminders** — a nudge when the timer runs while you don't (v2).
- **Timesheets with approval** — a weekly sheet plus sign-off (v3).
