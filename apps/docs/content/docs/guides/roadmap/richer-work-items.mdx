Work items in RyTask already cover the essentials. This page collects the planned
additions to the item itself — the small things that add up: more than one owner,
following without owning, files on the item, and editing many items at once without
the storage caps and tier-gating common elsewhere.

What's planned:

- **Multi-assignee** — more than one owner per item, for pairing (MVP scope).
- **Watchers / subscribers** — follow an item without owning it (MVP scope).
- **Per-item attachments** — files on items, without tight storage caps (MVP scope).
- **Bulk edit** — select many items and change them together (MVP scope).
- **Issue templates** — pre-filled item shapes (v2).
- **Issue types** — typed items like bug, feature, chore (v2).
- **Convert, split, and merge** — restructure items as work evolves (v2).
- **Recurring tasks** — auto-recreate items on a schedule (v2).
