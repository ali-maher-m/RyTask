Every SaaS tracker paywalls enterprise identity — the "SSO tax" is an industry standard.
RyTask's plan is the opposite: when SSO, SAML, and SCIM land, they will be **free in the
self-hosted edition**, like every other feature. The same wave brings the operational
maturity self-hosters ask for: telemetry hooks, proof of scale, and eventually a
first-class Kubernetes story.

What's planned:

- **SSO / SAML / SCIM** — enterprise identity, free in self-host (v2).
- **Observability hooks** — logs, metrics, and traces for your monitoring stack (v2).
- **Horizontal scalability** — scale out under load; architected for it from day one (v2).
- **Performance budgets and load tests in CI** — regressions caught before release (v2).
- **Helm / Kubernetes chart** — cluster deployment (v3).
