RyTask plans **cycles** — time-boxed batches of work (call them sprints or iterations if
you prefer). The detail that matters most is **auto-rollover**: when a cycle ends,
unfinished items carry forward to the next one automatically, instead of demanding a
manual grooming session. Teams that use Linear consistently name this as the feature
they love; almost everywhere else it is busywork.

What's planned:

- **Time-boxed cycles** — recurring work batches with start and end dates (MVP scope).
- **Auto-rollover of unfinished work** — incomplete items move forward on their own (MVP scope).
