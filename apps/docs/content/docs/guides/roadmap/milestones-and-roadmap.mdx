RyTask plans **milestones**: named delivery targets with fixed dates that give a project
shape beyond its day-to-day item list. Higher-altitude planning — a roadmap view across
quarters and initiatives — follows in a later wave, since those are the features most
often paywalled elsewhere (Jira and Asana gate their roadmaps).

What's planned:

- **Milestones** — named delivery targets per project (MVP scope).
- **Milestone dates** — fixed target markers visible on planning views (MVP scope).
- **Roadmap view** — a quarter or initiative timeline (v2).
- **Initiatives, goals, and OKRs** — company-level grouping above projects (v2).
- **Project status updates** — on track / at risk / off track posts (v2).
