One thing first: this page is **not** about the time reports. The flagship
"where did my time go?" reporting — planned versus interruption — is a separate effort
that is in progress right now; see [reporting](/docs/guides/users/reporting) for that.

This page covers the rest of the planned reporting surface: the sprint and flow charts
that competitors routinely gate (ClickUp puts burndown behind its Business tier, Jira
paywalls dashboards). RyTask plans to ship them free, building on one aggregation engine
shared with views and filters.

What's planned:

- **Burndown / burnup charts** — sprint progress over time (MVP scope).
- **Velocity** — throughput per cycle (MVP scope).
- **Cycle time / lead time** — flow metrics (v2).
- **Cumulative flow diagram** — work in progress over time (v2).
- **Custom dashboards and widgets** — composable charts (v2).
- **Workload / capacity** — who is overloaded (v2).
- **Saved and scheduled report exports** — email, CSV, PDF (v2).
- **Cross-project portfolio rollup** — multi-project status (v3).
