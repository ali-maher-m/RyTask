<ComingSoon tier="v2" />

RyTask already has the permission basics: roles, organization isolation, and
project-level access. This page covers the planned finer-grained layer — the controls
that competitors usually paywall (custom roles are paid in ClickUp and Plane; audit logs
are gated in Jira and Plane).

What's planned:

- **Custom roles and granular permissions** — fine-grained capability grants beyond the built-in tiers (v2).
- **Audit log** — who did what, when, across the organization (v2).
- **Field and record-level security** — hide specific fields or records by role (v3).
