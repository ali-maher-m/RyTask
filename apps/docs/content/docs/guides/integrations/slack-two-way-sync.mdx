<ComingSoon tier="MVP scope" />

What works **today**: the `/task` slash command creates a RyTask item from Slack in
seconds, a guided modal helps you fill in the details, and you get an ephemeral
acknowledgment with a link to the new item. See the [Slack guide](/docs/guides/slack)
for setup and usage.

This page covers the rest of the planned Slack story (differentiator D2): making Slack a
true two-way surface rather than a one-way capture funnel. The aim is to keep the
conversation and the work connected — without turning your channels into notification
noise.

What's planned:

- **@bot mention capture** — mention the bot in a thread to turn the conversation into a task (MVP scope).
- **Two-way sync** — status changes and comments flow back to the originating Slack thread (MVP scope).
- **Smart, low-noise notifications** — inform without spamming the channel (MVP scope).
- **Channel-to-project routing** — map Slack channels to RyTask projects for intake (v2).
