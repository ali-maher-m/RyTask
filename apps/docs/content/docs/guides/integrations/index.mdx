title: Other integrations
description: What connects to RyTask today, and which integrations are planned next.
---

Today, one integration is live: **Slack capture**. You can create RyTask items from Slack
with the `/task` slash command and a guided modal — see the
[Slack guide](/docs/guides/slack) for setup and usage.

The pages in this section describe integrations that are **planned but not built yet**.
They are honest roadmap descriptions: no behavior on these pages exists in the product
today, and details may change before they ship.

- [GitHub](/docs/guides/integrations/github) — branch, PR, and commit linking with auto-close and status sync.
- [Email notifications](/docs/guides/integrations/email-notifications) — outgoing email for invites, resets, and activity.
- [Outbound webhooks](/docs/guides/integrations/outbound-webhooks) — signed event payloads pushed to your URLs.
- [Slack two-way sync](/docs/guides/integrations/slack-two-way-sync) — the rest of the Slack story beyond capture.

A few more are planned further out and don't have their own pages yet:

- **Email-to-task** <StatusBadge status="coming-soon" /> — forward an email to create an item (planned for v2).
- **Zapier / n8n connectors** <StatusBadge status="coming-soon" /> — no-code glue to other tools (planned for v2).
- **OAuth app platform** <StatusBadge status="coming-soon" /> — a third-party integration ecosystem (planned for v3).
