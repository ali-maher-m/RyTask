Two tutorials take you from nothing to confident:

1. **[Quickstart](/docs/tutorials/quickstart)** — self-host RyTask with Docker Compose, create
   your organization, add a project, capture your first task, and log time on it. About 15
   minutes, no prior knowledge assumed.
2. **[Your first week](/docs/tutorials/first-week)** — the everyday rhythm: capture from Slack,
   track time against an estimate, read the plan-vs-actual meter, and connect an AI agent over
   MCP.

If you only want to look something up, head to the [Reference](/docs/reference) instead.
