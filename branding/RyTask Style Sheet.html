Spacing, radius, shadow & motion

A 4px grid, small radii for precision, and tight warm-ink shadows — elevation is a whisper, never a cloud. Motion is fast and calm with no bounce: keyboard-first means snappy.

Guardrails

Never do this

The line between "calm and product-grade" and "generic AI app" is mostly a list of restraints.

No decorative gradientsNo purple-to-blue hero washes, no gradient buttons or text. Flat fills only; gradient is reserved for the time meter, if ever.
No glassmorphism or heavy blurOne subtle backdrop blur on the sticky bar; never frosted cards, modals, or nav.
No floaty drop shadowsElevation stays tight and warm-ink. No big soft colored glows under cards or buttons.
No jargon or hype copy“Synergy”, “supercharge”, “revolutionary” fail the non-technical-teammate test. Plain, kind, direct.
No proportional figuresEvery time, estimate, count or ID is tabular mono. Columns must never shift as numbers change.
No emoji as UI chromeLucide icons carry meaning. Emoji only inside user content, never as buttons, status, or nav.
No off-palette colorOnly Sunbeam, Honey, Stone and the three semantic hues (plus one indigo for info / in-review). No neon, no random extra hue, no gradients.
No big radiiNothing over 18px except pills. Soft, bubbly cards read as consumer-cute, not focused.
